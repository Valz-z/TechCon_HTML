History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small meetup for local technology enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest tech conferences worldwide, attracting innovators from various fields.

Mission

Our mission is to inspire innovation and collaboration in technology by connecting thought leaders, developers, and industry pioneers. We aim to foster a community that drives technological progress responsibly and inclusively.
